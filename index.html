About Me

Hello, my name is Barat Ali. I specialize in character rigging for films, commercials, and video games, with over seven years of experience in the industry. My expertise includes biped, quadruped, bird, reptile, creature, and mechanical rigging.

My journey began in an isolated village in Afghanistan during the civil war, where I faced significant challenges, including a battle with polio. At 16, I arrived in Australia as a refugee, which marked a turning point in my life. Embracing new opportunities, I pursued my education and earned a Bachelor of Animation from Griffith University.

My career in the film industry began as a Rigger, working with renowned companies like Animal Logic. This path has been one of resilience and passion, transforming my challenges into strengths and driving my professional success. I am currently working at Animal Logic in Sydney, Australia, where I continue to hone my skills and contribute to creative projects.
